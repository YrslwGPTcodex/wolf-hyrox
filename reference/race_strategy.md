# Race Strategy — HYROX Sydney 1:15

> **Race-day pacing logic. Updated when phase changes or tactical model evolves.**

---

## Core Principle

Do not race early. Build the race from control.

The race will not be won by aggression. It will be lost by paying too much, too early.

---

## Primary Strategy

- Keep early run segments controlled
- Do not pay too much before BBJ and Wall Balls
- Preserve shoulder freshness as long as possible
- Use sleds as strength stations, not panic stations
- Protect rhythm before chasing pace

---

## Running Strategy

### Early Race (runs 1–3)
- Open controlled
- Do not run the first half like a stand-alone race
- Goal: arrive at stations usable, not impressive
- Target pace: 4:10–4:15 / km

### Mid Race (runs 4–6)
- Hold stable rhythm
- Avoid emotional surges
- Run serves station quality, not the other way around
- Target pace: 4:15–4:25 / km (after stations 3–5)

### Late Race (runs 7–8)
- Squeeze pace only when wall balls are still alive
- If wall balls are at risk, the run was too expensive earlier
- Last 1 km run before WB: target 4:15–4:20 with HR in 150–160 zone

---

## Station Priorities

### Sled Push (Station 2)
- Becoming a weapon (245 kg working at 18 m, race-distance 204 kg in 1:22)
- Use composure and pressure
- No panic steps
- Target time: 1:20–1:40

### Sled Pull (Station 3)
- Stay smooth
- Avoid jerky wasted movement
- Repeatability matters more than drama
- Target time: 2:30–3:00 (current sub-3:00 demonstrated)

### Burpee Broad Jumps (Station 4)
- **Current primary limiter**
- Economical rhythm; no random jumps
- Stay organized under fatigue
- **Round 1 pacing discipline:** target steady, NOT fast (lesson from W20 THU)
- Target time: 4:00–4:30

### Row Erg (Station 5)
- Already strong (1000 m in 4:06 demonstrated)
- Steady pace; controlled breathing
- Target time: 3:45–4:00

### Farmers Carry (Station 6)
- Working: 32+32 kg DB unbroken 320 m (race standard 200 m)
- No drama, just walk
- Target time: 1:30–2:00

### Sandbag Lunges (Station 7)
- 28+28 kg DB / 100 m at race
- Posture and discipline
- No rushed ugly steps
- Target time: 4:00–5:00

### Wall Balls (Station 8 — FINAL)
- **Main limiter, main race-defining station**
- Everything before WB must protect WB capacity
- Race target: 100 reps
- Ideal execution: 70+ UB then small break to finish
- Realistic execution: 50 + 50 with 20 sec reset
- Both options fit 1:15:00

## Main Rule for 1:15

A 1:15 race will not be lost because of lack of courage.

It will be lost by paying too much too early.
